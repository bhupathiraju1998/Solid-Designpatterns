🧱 Structural Patterns

Structural patterns focus on:

“HOW objects/classes are organized and connected.”

They solve problems related to:

simplifying systems
reducing coupling
wrapping complexity
composing objects together
Examples of structural patterns
Pattern	Purpose
Facade	simplified interface
Adapter	convert incompatible interfaces
Decorator	add behavior dynamically
Composite	tree-like object structures
Proxy	controlled access
Bridge	separate abstraction from implementation
